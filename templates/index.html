About Me

Michael is a data scientist, software developer, and musician who combines his passions and skills to create Mashmia.com, a platform that generates personalized music recommendations. He has over two years of experience as a founder, product developer, and software engineer in the music technology industry, where he leverages machine learning, artificial intelligence, and human-computer interaction to deliver innovative solutions.

Previously, Michael worked as a technology research consultant for GLG, where he helped F50 tech companies with M&A, product strategy, and product development by building and leading consulting teams. Michael holds a BS in Mathematics and Anthropology from Johns Hopkins University, where he was an active leader and advisor in the student government association. He speaks three languages and plays seven instruments.
